## По принадлежности

### Армия Данделия
- [[Маэв, Ведьма Грёз]] — исследователь грёз, проникает в сны врагов и союзников
- [[Ведьма Забвения]] (Тиша) — ~450 лет, одна из старейших; стирает память; инструмент [[Персонажи/Демоны/Демон Отражений\|Демона Отражений]] (не знает об этом)
- [[Ведьма Голода]] (Голод) — ~200 лет, рождена после [[Лор/Серебряная Чума\|Серебряной Чумы]]; вызывает неутолимый голод
- [[Ведьма Ржавчины]] (Ирра) — ~300 лет, из [[Локации/Королевство Хакирия/История Королевства Хакирия\|Хакирии]]; разрушает металл прикосновением
- [[Ведьма Истины|Верена (Ведьма Истины)]] — ~500 лет, одна из старейших; приближённая и советница Данделия; подарила [[Персонажи/Игроки/Агнейр Делир|Агнейру]] Амулет Истины; ведёт 500-летнее расследование системной лжи в мире
- [[Ведьма Падения]] (Нирэн) — ~180 лет; чувствует падение всего сущего; пережила поглощение Деймоса
- [[Ведьма Жертвы]] (Агна) — ~400 лет; идеальный ритуальный компонент; бесконечная жертва для любых ритуалов

### Независимые
- [[Золотая ведьма Беатриче]] — устроительница балов в [[Лор/Кария|Карии]], покровительница искусств
- [[Аврора, Ведьма Уныния]] — 50 лет, [[Персонажи/Расы/Еналы|Еналка]]; нанята [[Союз Свободных Племён\|ССП]] на 100 лет; чувствует тоску [[Лор/Толубра, Змея Превращения\|Толубры]]
- [[Миланида]] — воскрешена из Бездны, член династии [[Эрр'Кин|Эрр'Кина]]
- [[Ведьма Порога]] (Кирэль) — ~350 лет; живёт на краю [[Лор/Провал Карии|Провала Карии]]; чувствует стены тюрьмы [[Персонажи/НПС/Лиан|Лиан]]
- [[Ведьма Долга]] (Валтис) — ~270 лет; видит все обязательства; перемещается между [[Локации/Элзия/Элзия|Элзией]] и [[Лор/Кария|Карией]]
- [[Ведьма Пепла]] (Зола) — ~200 лет; читает историю уничтоженного; хранит фрагменты записной книжки Эрика Пустого
- [[Ведьма Одиночества]] — ~120 лет, [[Персонажи/Расы/Еналы|Еналка]]; движется к [[Лор/Провал Карии|Провалу Карии]] и [[Персонажи/НПС/Лиан|Лиан]]
- [[Самара, Ведьма Последнего Вздоха|Самара]] — ~500+ лет, одна из древнейших; хирургия души; заключает пакты на последний вздох

### Погибшие / Пропавшие
- [[Ведьма Тумана]] (Сайра) — ~250 лет на момент исчезновения; растворилась в тумане [[Локации/Торсия/География Торсии|Гнилых Топей]]; фрагменты сознания ещё существуют
- [[Ведьма Гниения]] (Морра) — ~420 лет на момент смерти; рассыпалась в труху; наставница [[Маэв, Ведьма Грёз|Маэв]]; подозревала о создателе ведьм

| Связь | Суть |
|-------|------|
| **Забвения ↔ Пепла** | Комплементарные якоря: одна стирает, другая читает остатки |
| **Забвения ↔ Демон Отражений** | Тиша — его инструмент для стирания знаний о Лиан (не знает) |

| **Падения → Данделий** | Нирэн чувствует его «потенциал падения» и молчит |
| **Долга ↔ Контракты** | Валтис видит скелет демонической контрактной системы |
| **Пепла → Гниения** | Зола может прочитать останки Морры и узнать тайны |

| **Тумана → Маэв** | Единственная подруга; её исчезновение — травма Маэв |
| **Беатриче ↔ Все ведьмы** | Организует шабаши — единственные регулярные собрания ведьм в мире |

| **Беатриче ↔ Порога** | Кирэль приходит на каждый шабаш, стоит в дверях, видит шабаш как храм порогов |

| **Истины → Данделий** | Единственный человек без конструктивной лжи; Верена — его «тихая правда» |
| **Истины ↔ Беатриче** | Тихое уважение. Подарила камень истинного цвета; Беатриче не использовала на статуях |

## Непримиримые конфликты

| Пара | Суть конфликта | Характер |
|------|---------------|----------|
| **[[Ведьма Ржавчины\|Ирра]] ↔ [[Ведьма Забвения\|Тиша]]** | Тиша стирает руны Ирры (300 лет документации). Точность vs. стирание. Данделий разводит по разным лагерям | Ирра — холодная ненависть. Тиша — не помнит проблемы |
| **[[Ведьма Долга\|Валтис]] ↔ [[Миланида]]** | Хаос Миланиды рвёт нити обязательств Валтис. Физическая боль при сближении | Валтис — боль и осуждение. Миланида — презрительное безразличие |
| **[[Ведьма Истины\|Верена]] ↔ [[Маэв, Ведьма Грёз\|Маэв]]** | Истина рассеивает грёзы, грёзы ослабевают рядом с истиной. Данделий использует поочерёдно | Верена — молчаливая неприязнь. Маэв — исследовательский интерес |
| **[[Самара, Ведьма Последнего Вздоха\|Самара]] ↔ [[Ведьма Долга\|Валтис]]** | Пакты Самары неравноценны; Валтис видит перекос. Извращение vs. пуризм концепции долга | Валтис — принципиальное отвращение. Самара — насмешка |

## Промпты для генерации портретов (Nano Banana)

> **Единый стиль:** Dark fantasy portrait painting in the style of 17th-century Flemish masters. Oil on canvas texture, dramatic Rembrandt chiaroscuro lighting, rich warm shadows with a single light source. Half-body composition. The subject has long pointed horizontal elf-like ears (Terr'Koir racial trait). Ornate dark gilded frame border. Moody occult atmosphere. Highly detailed face, hands, and textures. Muted colour palette with one dominant accent colour unique to each witch.

---

### 1. Маэв, Ведьма Грёз

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A young woman with permanently closed eyes, serene otherworldly expression. Long flowing rose-pink hair that floats weightlessly around her like underwater seaweed, some strands wrapping around the frame itself. Long pointed horizontal elf ears partially hidden by hair. Wearing a faded pink gown that seems to bleed into the background. Objects near her lose their colour, becoming desaturated. A faint pale pink glow emanates from beneath her closed eyelids. Dramatic chiaroscuro, single warm light source from above. Dominant colour: dusty rose pink. Ornate gilded frame. Moody dreamy atmosphere, as if the painting itself is falling asleep.
```

### 2. Золотая ведьма Беатриче

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A young playful girl with a mischievous half-smile, looking directly at the viewer with sharp knowing eyes. Red silk ribbon bow in her styled hair. Wearing an elegant modern-cut blazer over a fine dress, anachronistic and bold. Long pointed horizontal elf ears. Golden butterflies surround her, some landing on her shoulders and the gilded frame. Behind her — glimpses of golden statues in a grand ballroom. Dramatic chiaroscuro, warm candlelight glow. Dominant colour: rich gold. Ornate gilded frame decorated with tiny golden butterfly motifs. Opulent baroque atmosphere.
```

### 3. Миланида

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A young woman (~18) with wild unconventional appearance, vibrant and alive despite an aura of chaos. Long prominent horizontal elf ears. Holding a crooked wooden walking cane topped with a serpent head biting a glowing red crystal. Her expression is warm but with an edge of recklessness. Faint crackling lightning traces along her skin. Behind her — swirling void of absolute darkness (the Abyss), from which faint shapes emerge. Dramatic chiaroscuro, the red crystal providing a secondary light source. Dominant colour: deep crimson against abyssal black. Ornate gilded frame with cracks running through it.
```

### 4. Ведьма Истины

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A woman of indeterminate age with an unnervingly direct gaze that seems to see through the viewer. Eyes clear as glass, reflecting pure white light. Face perfectly symmetrical, features sharp and precise, no softness. Long pointed horizontal elf ears. Wearing austere dark robes. Around her neck — a faintly glowing amulet of layered crystalline truth. Her shadow on the wall behind her shows a different pose than her body. Dramatic chiaroscuro, harsh unforgiving light from the front. Dominant colour: cold white against deep shadow. Ornate gilded frame. Atmosphere of interrogation, of being seen completely.
```

### 5. Ведьма Забвения (Тиша)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A frail teenage girl with tangled ash-grey hair hiding her long horizontal elf ears. Pale grey almost white eyes with uneven pupils — left larger than right. Skin translucent, bluish veins visible at the temples. Wearing an oversized ill-fitting military jacket. Her hands clutch a rosary made of 47 human teeth on a sinew thread, fingers mid-fidget. Parts of the painting itself appear erased — sections of the background are blank canvas, as if the portrait is forgetting itself. Dramatic chiaroscuro, dim uncertain light. Dominant colour: washed-out grey. Ornate gilded frame with faded illegible inscriptions. Haunting atmosphere of absence.
```

### 6. Ведьма Голода (Голод)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A very tall gaunt woman with long unkempt grey hair falling to her waist. Sunken eyes the colour of scorched earth, staring with unblinking intensity at something beyond the frame. Prominent cheekbones, sharp jawline, collarbones visible beneath skin. Long horizontal elf ears with cracked dry skin at the tips. Bare shoulders visible — she wears no clothing, only a rough cloak draped carelessly over one shoulder by someone else. Mouth slightly open as if singing a silent dirge. The painted food on a nearby table appears to rot and wither. Dramatic chiaroscuro, cold harsh light. Dominant colour: burnt umber and bone. Ornate gilded frame. Atmosphere of famine, of endless emptiness.
```

### 7. Ведьма Ржавчины (Ирра)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. An elderly woman with a worn face, wrinkles resembling cracks in old metal. Long knotty fingers with rust-brown stains on the skin, brownish flaking nails — hands prominently displayed. Dull rust-red hair streaked with grey. Eyes the colour of oxidised copper: greenish-brown with amber flecks. Long horizontal elf ears covered in fine crack patterns like ancient bronze. Dressed in leather and rough cloth, everything fastened with knots and laces — no metal whatsoever. She is scratching indecipherable corrosion-runes into the wall beside her. The gilded frame itself shows creeping rust at the edges. Dramatic chiaroscuro, warm amber light. Dominant colour: rust orange. Atmosphere of slow inevitable decay.
```

### 8. Ведьма Тумана (Сайра)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A short thin-boned woman whose features are impossible to focus on — the paint itself seems smudged, blurred, as if the artist's hand trembled or the subject moved. Ash-grey hair floats weightlessly around her head despite no wind. Light grey almost transparent eyes like windows into an empty house. Long horizontal elf ears barely visible, translucent like jellyfish. Wearing grey shapeless garments — part dress, part cloak, part nothing. Dense fog pours from the bottom of the painting, obscuring her lower body entirely. The background dissolves into pure white mist. Dramatic chiaroscuro fighting against the fog. Dominant colour: pearl grey. Ornate gilded frame beaded with condensation. Atmosphere of dissolution, of being between.
```

### 9. Ведьма Гниения (Морра)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. An ancient twisted woman with skin covered in dark spots resembling mould. Knotted joints like the roots of a rotting tree. Long grey-green hair like moss on stone, matted and tangled, reaching the ground. Deep-set dark brown eyes — the only living part of her face, sharp and attentive and wise. Long horizontal elf ears darkened and cracked like old bark. Wearing decaying cloth that barely holds together, brown and riddled with holes. Around her — painted mushrooms sprouting from the frame, tiny insects, the beauty of decomposition. Smell almost visible as a green-brown haze. Dramatic chiaroscuro, warm earthy light as after rain. Dominant colour: forest brown-green. Ornate gilded frame overgrown with painted lichen. Atmosphere of compost, of transformation, of peace.
```

### 10. Аврора, Ведьма Уныния

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A very young woman (18) with enormous frightened doe-like brown eyes, wide open as if in perpetual shock. Messy dull green hair with strands falling across her face. Long prominent horizontal elf ears (Enal heritage), slightly drooping. Hunched posture, shoulders curled inward, leaning on a wooden serpent-shaped walking cane with a glowing red crystal in the snake's open mouth. Wearing a shapeless grey sack-like garment, but on her feet — incongruously bright red leather boots. The entire painting appears desaturated, colours muted and fading, as if joy itself has been drained from the pigments. A single tear track on one cheek. Dramatic chiaroscuro, grey diffused light like an overcast day. Dominant colour: muted grey-green with red accents. Ornate gilded frame. Atmosphere of quiet hopelessness, of rain that never stops.
```

### 11. Ведьма Падения (Нирэн)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A short stocky woman built like a soldier — wide shoulders, powerful stance, low centre of gravity. Black short choppy hair roughly cut by knife. Dark eyes looking downward, never up — she stares at the bottom edge of the frame. Long horizontal elf ears, heavy and slightly drooping. Tanned weathered skin covered in small scars. Wearing a practical leather jacket tightly strapped with buckles and laces, everything secured to prevent falling. Behind her — a night sky filled with falling stars (burning asteroids), each trailing fire. Objects in the painting subtly tilt and lean as if gravity pulls them sideways. Dramatic chiaroscuro, cold starlight from above. Dominant colour: midnight blue and ember orange. Ornate gilded frame slightly tilted. Atmosphere of vertigo, of inevitability.
```

### 12. Ведьма Порога (Кирэль)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A tall unnaturally narrow woman — compressed like a silhouette in a doorframe. Long straight ash-coloured hair parted exactly down the middle. Heterochromia: left eye grey, right eye brown — two worlds meeting. Skin pale on the left side of her face, sun-tanned on the right. Long horizontal elf ears stretched sideways like directional arrows. Wearing a long grey seamless dress belted with rope, fabric hanging like a door curtain. She stands precisely in a stone doorway, one foot on each side of the threshold, framed by the arch. The left side of the painting is dark interior, the right side is bright exterior. Dramatic chiaroscuro split down the middle. Dominant colour: stone grey with contrasting warm/cool halves. Ornate gilded frame shaped like a doorway arch. Atmosphere of liminality, of eternal transition.
```

### 13. Ведьма Долга (Валтис)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A woman of middle years with perfect rigid posture — spine straight as a scale's beam, not a millimetre of lean. Dark chestnut hair pulled into a severe tight bun, not a single strand loose. Grey-steel eyes that weigh and measure the viewer. Face perfectly symmetrical, expressionless but not cold — balanced. Long horizontal elf ears pressed close to the head, nearly invisible. Wearing a dark formal dress to the ankles, a thick shawl on shoulders. At her belt — a leather satchel with a worn leather-bound book of recorded debts. Faint translucent threads connect from her to things outside the frame — obligations made visible. Dramatic chiaroscuro, even measured light. Dominant colour: steel grey and legal-ink black. Ornate gilded frame. Atmosphere of a courtroom, of ledgers and unbreakable oaths.
```

### 14. Ведьма Пепла (Зола)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A short woman who seems to fade into the background — an overall impression of grey. Short ashy hair that leaves a powdery grey residue. Pale transparent eyes with irises that look sun-bleached, gazing through objects rather than at them. Tiny almost invisible horizontal elf ears covered in fine grey dust. Her most striking feature: darkened hands with fingertips that are nearly black, as if charred from touching a thousand burned things. Dressed in grey fabric — any colour she wears has faded. Painted ash drifts through the composition like snow. The painting's own colours seem to be fading at the edges, as if the portrait is slowly turning to ash. Dramatic chiaroscuro, dim smoky light. Dominant colour: ash grey and charcoal black. Ornate gilded frame dusted with a thin layer of painted soot. Atmosphere of aftermath, of everything already over.
```

### 15. Ведьма Одиночества

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A young woman (Enal race) — short, thin, curled in on herself with shoulders forward and arms pressed to sides, occupying as little space as possible. Long dark hair hanging like a curtain across her face, hiding her features. Behind the hair — large dark empty eyes, not dead but vacant, like windows of an abandoned house. Long horizontal elf ears (Enal heritage) drooping downward. Dressed in mismatched rags and scraps tied with knots — no one gave her proper clothes. She sits alone in the exact centre of an enormous empty room — no furniture, no decoration, just vast empty space around a single small figure. The painted room stretches impossibly far in all directions. Dramatic chiaroscuro, a single distant light source emphasising the emptiness. Dominant colour: hollow dark brown and void black. Ornate gilded frame. Atmosphere of absolute isolation, of a room no one will ever enter.
```

### 16. Ведьма Жертвы (Агна)

```
Dark fantasy portrait painting, 17th-century Flemish master style, oil on canvas. A young woman (~17-18) of fragile delicate build — narrow shoulders, thin wrists, hollow collarbones. Pure white hair, not silver not grey but lamb-white, hanging straight and heavy to her waist, parted simply in the middle. Pale amber eyes, calm, empty, unblinking — the gaze of a statue, of something that has stopped expecting. Skin unnervingly flawless, porcelain-smooth, not a single scar or blemish despite implied centuries of suffering — a body that erases its own history. Long pointed horizontal elf ears, pale almost translucent, fine capillaries visible beneath the skin. Wearing a simple undyed linen shift dress, plain, unadorned, the kind of garment that would not be mourned if ruined. Her hands rest open, palms upward, in her lap — a gesture of offering, of surrender. Around her, faint glowing ritual circles and runic symbols bleed through the dark background, as if the painting itself is a magical sigil hungry for her. A single thin line of blood traces down her left palm into the darkness below. The gilded frame is pristine, untouched, perfect — as if it too has been restored to an original state. Dramatic chiaroscuro, soft sacrificial light from directly above like an altar lamp. Dominant colour: lamb white and altar-blood red. Atmosphere of sacred violence, of willing surrender, of something precious offered and consumed and offered again.
```
